Sea Travel: Not Advised for This Itinerary

While Port Blair has a seaport, government passenger ships from mainland India (Chennai, Kolkata, Visakhapatnam) are not a viable option for your Nov 27th arrival and Dec 1st departure.

Key Reasons Sea Travel is Impractical:

  • Long Journey: 50-60 hours (2-3 days) one-way. Arrival would be Nov 29th/30th.
  • Infrequent Sailings: Only a few sailings per month from each port.
  • Port Halt: Ships halt for 2-4 days in Port Blair, making a quick return unlikely.
  • Limited Time: You'd have 1-2 days at most in Port Blair.

Private ferry services (Makruzz, Green Ocean, etc.) operate only for inter-island travel within the Andamans (e.g., to Havelock/Neil Island) and do not connect to mainland India.

Conclusion: For this short, specific itinerary, air travel is the only realistic way to maximize your time in Port Blair.

Essential Booking & Travel Tips

Book in Advance & Use Real-Time Search

Flight prices are dynamic. Use search engines like Expedia, Skyscanner, Kayak, or MakeMyTrip for current fares for Nov 27 - Dec 1. Book 2-3 months ahead if possible, as this is peak season.

Airport Arrival Times

Arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3-4 hours before international flights. For inter-island ferries from Port Blair, arrive at the jetty 45-60 minutes prior to departure.

Consider Mid-Week Travel

While your dates are fixed, generally, flying mid-week can sometimes be cheaper than on weekends. Booking on certain days (e.g., Sunday) might also yield better prices.

Identity Proof

Always carry valid government-issued photo identity proof for all travel, including flights and any potential ferry bookings.

About Veer Savarkar Int'l Airport (IXZ)

Veer Savarkar International Airport (IATA: IXZ) is the main gateway to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, located about 2 km south of Port Blair city.

Key Features:

  • Operates 24/7 for arrivals and departures.
  • International airport status.
  • Offers flexibility for flight timings.

Amenities:

  • ATMs & Currency Exchange
  • Baggage Handling
  • Cafes
  • Car Rental Services
  • No Wi-Fi available.

Its 24/7 operation is beneficial for tight schedules and connecting flights with varied timings.
