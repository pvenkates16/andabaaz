Sightseeing Highlights (Recap & Details)

  • Mysore Palace (Amba Vilas Palace): A must-see, illuminated on Sundays and public holidays (check exact schedule for November).
  • Brindavan Gardens: Famous terraced gardens with musical fountains (best visited in the evening).
  • Chamundi Hills & Sri Chamundeshwari Temple: Panoramic views of Mysore and a significant temple.
  • Jaganmohan Palace Art Gallery: Houses a remarkable collection of paintings, including those by Raja Ravi Varma.
  • Karanji Lake: Serene lake with a walk-through aviary and boating options.
  • Mysore Zoo (Sri Chamarajendra Zoological Gardens): One of India's oldest and most well-maintained zoos.
  • Devraj Market: A vibrant local market for a true sensory experience of Mysore.
